    Variance Swap Replication: From Itô's Lemma to a Tradeable Portfolio

    方差互换复制：从伊藤引理到可交易组合

    Derives the pathwise variance swap replication identity from Itô's lemma, decomposes the log contract into a static strip of vanilla options via the Carr–Madan spanning formula, and verifies the construction with a Monte Carlo backtest under both GBM and Heston dynamics. Quantifies the four practical gaps, finite strike grid, discrete rebalancing, jump risk, and funding cost, that separate theory from real QIS product pricing.
